FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

The site won't let me order anything

Please send us an email at kaysklosetonline@gmail.com with the items you'd like to purchase and we will set up a custom order for you.

Something won't load?

Please send us an email at kaysklosetonline@gmail.com with a description of the problem and a screenshot, if possible.

How do I get in contact with you?

You can reach us by email at kaysklosetonline@gmail.com.

Where are you located?

Chicago, Illinois.

Which methods of payments can I use?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and Paypal.

Do you restock items? How often?

We do not restock items often. In the event that we do, it will be shared across our social media, or you may visit our site again for updates.

Do you ship to..?

Great news! We ship worldwide.

How much and how long does it take?

Shipping within the U.S. generally takes 1-3 business days, after 3-4 days of processing. All U.S. orders $150 and up will receive free shipping. Express shipments vary in price by destination and can be calculated on the checkout page.

How do I check the status of my order?

Typically, once an order is made, we will send you tracking information to your email. In the event that you do not receive any tracking info, you can email us at kaysklosetonline@gmail.com to check on the status of your order.

Do you make custom orders?

At this time, we do not offer custom orders.

Do you offer different sizes?

Unfortunately, we carry limited sizes.

Something wrong with your order?

If a problems occurs with your order, please contact us as quickly as possible by emailing kaysklosetonline@gmail.com. Once your order has been placed, we cannot make changes to your order.

Can I return a sale item?

All sales are final and cannot be returned.

How do i cancel an order?

Please contact us at kaysklosetonline@gmail.com if there is an issue with your order. Once your order has shipped, we can no longer makes changes, cancel, or refund your order.

How do i return/exchange an item(s)?

To return an unworn item, please send us an email at kaysklosetonline@gmail.com and mail the item with an insured shipping service to : Kemah Johnson Order # 7824 S. South Shore Dr. Apt # 2D Chicago, Il 60649
